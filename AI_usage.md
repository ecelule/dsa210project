## Use of AI Tools

AI tools (specifically ChatGPT) are used as a supportive resource during the development of this project.

AI assistance is used in the following parts of the workflow:

- **Code debugging and implementation support:** to identify and resolve issues in data preprocessing, dataset merging, and the implementation of machine learning models (Linear Regression and KNN).
- **Conceptual understanding:** to better understand statistical and machine learning concepts such as multicollinearity, model evaluation (MSE, R²), feature scaling, and hyperparameter tuning.
- **Interpretation of results:** to assist in interpreting regression coefficients, residual plots, and model performance results.
- **Improving explanations:** to refine written explanations and improve clarity in markdown sections.

All final analysis, modeling decisions, and interpretations are independently understood, verified, and implemented by myself.
